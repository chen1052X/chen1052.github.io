About Me

After graduating from Newcastle University with a degree in journalism, I embarked on a journey to establish myself in the media landscape. Initially, I aimed to become a journalist, but I soon discovered my true calling in public relations, where I found greater professional opportunities. As my career evolved, I transitioned into copywriting, allowing me to blend creativity with strategic communication.

During the pandemic, I embraced freelancing, which provided me with the flexibility to work on diverse projects. However, the emergence of AI technologies disrupted my workflow, leading me to reevaluate my career path. Recognizing the need to adapt to this changing landscape, I sought new avenues for growth and learning.

This quest for innovation and development led me to enroll in an interactive media course at Algonquin College. Here, I aim to enhance my skills in digital storytelling and media production, preparing myself for a future that integrates traditional communication with cutting-edge technology. With each step, I remain committed to evolving as a professional and exploring the exciting intersections of media and technology in our increasingly digital world.
