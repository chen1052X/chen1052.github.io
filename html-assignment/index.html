About Me

After graduating from Newcastle University with a degree in journalism, I embarked on a journey to establish myself in the media landscape. Initially, I aimed to become a journalist, but I soon discovered my luck in public relations, where I found better professional opportunities. As my career evolved, I transitioned into copywriting, allowing me to blend creativity with strategic communication.

During the pandemic, I became a full-time freelancer, which offered me the flexibility to work on both writing and media anlysis. However, the emergence of AI technologies disrupted my workflow, leading me to reevaluate my career path. Recognizing the need to adapt to this changing landscape, I sought new space for growth and learning.

This quest for a renewed career opportunity led me to sign up for the interactive media course at Algonquin College. Here, I aim to retrain my skillset in media production and web development, preparing myself for a future that integrates traditional communication with technology.
