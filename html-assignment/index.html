About Me

Born and raised in China with an undying passion for language learning and a creative mind, I initially strove to train myself to be a bilingual journalist as a media student but found my way into the PR industry after graduation. My first job opportunity gained me a reputation in my team as a fast learner and professional copywriter, but it didn't offer a clear pathway for my future career outlook. So, I resigned from the company where I worked for two years and decided to try freelancing as a digital writer and media analyst.

Despite hitting many barriers in the first few months, which made me think I might eventually need to find a "real job" to secure a healthy income, the pandemic actually landed me in a fortunate position, which allowed me to work from home while earning more money than I expected. However, the rise of AI, coupled with the gloomy post-pandemic economic landscape that hit many businesses around the world, has created another turning point for my work, as I came to realize that it will not provide an infinite number of gigs that are gradually taken over by LLMs. This is the reason why I am here, studying at Algonquin College, to see if I can unlock my talent in interactive media development.

As for my favorite quote, it really took me a while to choose one (sorry, not a big fan of motivational quotes). I eventually settled on two lines of lyrics from a song by Accusefive, a Taiwanese band. Though not motivational, these lyrics resonate deeply, capturing the essence of some people's experiences in romantic relationships. I chose these because I think the songwriter is very creative.

It goes like this:

Some are born to live without restraint,
where every greeting becomes a true love's claim.
Some are born to live for just one soul,
while all goodwill is turned away at the gate.
- Accusefive
